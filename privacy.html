Privacy Policy

Overview

At Shadowlark's Oracle, we believe in complete spiritual and digital autonomy. Our application is designed with a "Local First" philosophy.

Data Collection

The Shadowlark Oracle application (Windows and Android) does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All numerical evaluations, anagram generations, and tarot readings are processed locally on your device.

Offline Processing

Our localized cognitive core (LLM) operates entirely offline once downloaded. Your queries never leave your hardware. We do not track your questions, your results, or your usage patterns.
